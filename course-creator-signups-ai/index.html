How to Get More Course Signups With AI (Without Discounting or Spamming)

Short answer: stop slashing prices and blasting promos. Use AI to create a genuinely useful free sample — one lesson, a worksheet, a mini-course — that prospective students opt in for. It proves your value, grows a list you own, and lets the full course sell on merit. Discounts train buyers to wait; useful free content makes them want in.

Why discounting and spam quietly hurt you

Constant discounts teach your audience that your course isn't worth full price — so they wait for the next sale. Spamming promos burns your list and your sender reputation. Neither builds the trust that actually sells a course. A free, genuinely useful sample does the opposite: it demonstrates the transformation before anyone pays.

The attraction system for course creators

  1. Attract: AI helps you answer the question your future students search before they buy — "how do I [outcome the course delivers]." Publish one truly helpful piece.
  2. Capture: offer a real slice of the course (a lesson or worksheet) behind a simple opt-in. One clear promise, one yes.
  3. Follow up: an automated welcome sequence — deliver the sample, share a quick win, then invite them into the full course as the natural next step.
  4. Offer: the paid course sells because they've already experienced the value — no discount needed.
Keep it honest: don't invent student results or fake scarcity ("only 2 seats left" when there aren't). Real outcomes and a genuinely useful sample convert better and keep you compliant.

Start this week

  • Pick the one outcome your best students rave about.
  • Have AI help you package one lesson or worksheet as a free sample.
  • Put it behind an opt-in; set one welcome email that delivers a quick win.
  • Share it where your audience already gathers — helpfully, not as spam.
